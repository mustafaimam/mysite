About

SooHacks is a hackathon hosted at Algoma University. Join us in the Soo for a weekend of hacking.

Students gather to build software or hardware in teams of 1-4 over 36 hours. Learn from the best at our workshops, create something awesome, and share it with your new friends. Meet celebrity judges and earn sweet prizes.
